EVENT DESIGN SERVICES

At Kate Ryan Design we transform blank spaces into sophisticated, luxury environments. We bring emotion and story to your event. We do so by combining the imagination and vision of our client with the infinite creativity and years of professional experience of Katie Easley and team. Our Design Process is unique and client driven.

Each event starts with building a relationship with you. We respect and listen to your ideas while we explore the world of custom design & develop new concepts exclusively for you. We collaborate with the industry's best talent to make choices relating to the overall styling, décor, accessories and items which will create the ambiance & atmosphere of your celebration. Our process is simple, once contracted, a design board and presentation are created custom for you to explain all the details and design specifics for your wedding or event. Our production team will begin planning to execute your event flawlessly with meticulous attention to detail. The final stages of your event design involve out installation and operations stage where we are dedicated to the smallest details, right down to the last chair placement and napkin floral detail.

As clients, you choose Kate Ryan Design because you value the absolute best…in custom design, unparalleled creativity, traditional floral artistry, modern ideas, trend setting details and the professionalism of an experienced design team. Our clients are busy, hard working professionals that trust us as design experts. We value the monies that you allocate to professional photography and videography. Therefore, all of our events are “magazine ready” upon completion and prior to the moment that you experience the transformed space for the first time.
